# Community Use Agreements (CUA) – nmap_report_generator

This document provides **formal Community Use Agreement (CUA) templates** for
organizations that wish to use [nmap_report_generator](https://github.com/Hjuanma/nmap_report_generator)
under the Common Sense License (CSL) v1.1.

These templates ensure:

- Ethical compliance
- Contributions to the commons
- Protection of user rights
- Fairness and transparency across all user types

The templates cover:

1. Nonprofit / Educational Institutions
2. Small Business / Cooperative
3. Large Corporate / Revenue-Based
4. Custom Use Cases

---

## Instructions for Use

1. **Identify your category** – Determine which type best fits your organization.
2. **Fill in the template fields** – Insert your organization's name, contacts,
   and contribution details where applicable.
3. **Sign the agreement** – Digitally or physically, by an authorized representative.
4. **Submit the agreement** – Send completed agreements to the maintainer via GitHub:
   https://github.com/Hjuanma or open an issue at
   https://github.com/Hjuanma/nmap_report_generator/issues
5. **Keep records** – Retain a copy for internal compliance.

## Template 1: Nonprofit / Educational Institution

**Eligibility:**
Nonprofits, schools, universities, and other educational or community-focused
organizations.

```
Community Use Agreement – Nonprofit / Educational

This Agreement is entered into between [Institution Name] ("User")
and the maintainer of nmap_report_generator ("Maintainer").

1. License Grant:
   User may use, modify, and distribute the Software for nonprofit,
   educational, or community-oriented purposes under CSL v1.1.

2. Contributions:
   Optional: User is encouraged to contribute improvements,
   documentation, or modules back to the project or a Commons Fund.

3. Reporting:
   User agrees to submit an annual acknowledgment of Software use
   to the Maintainer via GitHub.

4. Ethical Compliance:
   User affirms the Software will not be used for exploitative,
   surveillance, military, or environmentally harmful purposes.

5. Attribution:
   User will retain CSL license text and attribution in all copies.

Authorized Representative:
Name:      _______________________
Title:     _______________________
Signature: _______________________
Date:      _______________________
```

For-profit organizations with annual gross revenue under USD $10 million,
cooperatives, and social enterprises.

and the maintainer of nmap_report_generator ("Maintainer").

## Template 3: Large Corporate / Revenue-Based

**Eligibility:**
For-profit organizations with annual gross revenue of USD $10 million or more
(including subsidiaries and affiliates).

```
Community Use Agreement – Large Corporate

This Agreement is entered into between [Organization Name] ("User")
and the maintainer of nmap_report_generator ("Maintainer").

1. License Grant:
   User may use, modify, and distribute the Software under CSL v1.1
   only after executing this Agreement.

2. Contribution Requirement:
   - Minimum 13.37% of Gross Attributable Revenue generated through
     the Software; OR
   - Equivalent contribution in verified Labor Contribution (valued
     at fully loaded employee cost); OR
   - A hybrid combination of both.

3. Reporting:
   - Annual, auditable report of revenue or labor contributions.
   - Reports submitted to Maintainer or an agreed independent auditor.

4. Derivative Works:
   All modifications or derivative works must be released under CSL v1.1,
   unless verified contributions per Section 2 are made and documented.

5. Ethical Compliance:
   User affirms the Software will not be used in violation of CSL v1.1
   ethical-use restrictions (Section 11).

6. Audit & Verification:
   Maintainer may request documentation to verify contributions and
   revenue figures.

7. Termination:
   Non-compliance terminates all rights until fully remedied within
   30 days of written notice.

8. Attribution:
   Retain CSL license text and original author attribution in all copies.

Authorized Representative:
Name:      _______________________
Title:     _______________________
Signature: _______________________
Date:      _______________________
```

## Template 4: Custom Use Case / Other Organizations

For organizations that do not fit the above categories (e.g., government
agencies, foundations, international organizations):

**Instructions:**

1. Describe your organization and intended use.
2. Provide contact information and point of responsibility.
3. Propose a contribution structure (monetary, labor, or resource-based).
4. Submit for review by opening an issue at:
   https://github.com/Hjuanma/nmap_report_generator/issues
5. Maintainer will respond with a custom CUA ensuring ethical compliance
   and contribution alignment.

- Maintainer may **audit compliance** if needed to protect the commons.
